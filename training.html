Training Packs

Prepare without pressure.

Choose a simple preparation plan based on your comfort level. No prior running experience is required.

Beginner

Walking Plan

Best for participants who want to complete the event comfortably through walking.

01

15-20 minutes light walk, 3 times per week

02

20-25 minutes brisk walk, 3 times per week

03

30 minutes walk with short faster intervals

04

Practice a comfortable event pace

Run-Walk

Run-Walk Plan

Best for participants who want to alternate between jogging and walking.

01

1 minute jog, 2 minutes walk, repeat 6 times

02

2 minutes jog, 2 minutes walk, repeat 6 times

03

3 minutes jog, 1 minute walk, repeat 6 times

04

Maintain a comfortable run-walk rhythm

Runner

5K Preparation Plan

Best for participants who want to run most or all of the route.

01

Easy 2 KM run, 2-3 times per week

02

Easy 3 KM run with light stretching

03

4 KM run at a comfortable pace

04

Light 5K practice before race week

Train safely.

Start slowly, drink water, rest when needed, and avoid pushing through pain. The goal is to finish comfortably.
