Training Packs

Prepare without pressure.

These beginner-friendly plans are designed for walkers, casual participants, and runners who want to prepare steadily before race day.

Beginner

Walking Plan

Best for participants who want to complete the event comfortably through walking.

  • Week 1: 15–20 minutes light walk, 3 times per week
  • Week 2: 20–25 minutes brisk walk, 3 times per week
  • Week 3: 30 minutes walk with short faster intervals
  • Week 4: Practice a comfortable event pace

Run-Walk

Run-Walk Plan

Best for participants who want to alternate between jogging and walking.

  • Week 1: 1 minute jog, 2 minutes walk, repeat 6 times
  • Week 2: 2 minutes jog, 2 minutes walk, repeat 6 times
  • Week 3: 3 minutes jog, 1 minute walk, repeat 6 times
  • Week 4: Maintain a comfortable run-walk rhythm

Runner

5K Preparation Plan

Best for participants who want to run most or all of the route.

  • Week 1: Easy 2 KM run, 2–3 times per week
  • Week 2: Easy 3 KM run with light stretching
  • Week 3: 4 KM run at comfortable pace
  • Week 4: Light 5K practice before race week
